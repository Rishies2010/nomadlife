Communicate with ease

The Discord-MC Chat Mod

Our server has a mod installed, called the Discord-MC Chat Mod. For the players who are in our Discord Server, they can chat to other players in game through Discord.

This allows our players to talk to their friends online without ever need in to join the game, if they are in a situation where they cannot play the game.

Anti Cheat

Cheaters ? No problem!

They won't make it far. Our server has powerful mods like the Anti-Xray mod, etc. Anyone who is caught cheating is automatically banned by our server!

We want our server to have genuine players who work the hard and smart way, not the crooked way. Cheating is NOT ALLOWED on our server. Help us keep our server free of hackers by reporting them.

Efficient Speed

Less mods, less lags.

As described, our server runs on the bare minimums, and the only mods installed are Geyser and Discord-MC Chat, and a few Anti-cheats and optimization mods, which makes the server experience better for everyone.

This allows the server to boot quicker , run quicker with less lag, and most of all, provide a better experience to the players on our server. The server is smooth and steady.
